How to Keep Your Watch Looking Premium for Years

Published on March 8, 2026 by Glamtreasure Team

A good watch is more than an accessory. It is a daily style statement. Proper maintenance helps preserve shine, dial readability, and strap durability. Here are simple habits you can follow.

1. Clean Weekly With a Soft Cloth

After regular use, wipe the case and strap using a dry microfiber cloth. This removes sweat, dust, and skin oil that can make metallic surfaces dull over time.

2. Avoid Direct Perfume Contact

Spray perfume before wearing your watch. Direct chemical contact can affect polish and strap quality.

3. Protect Accessories From Moisture

Store shades, belts, and wallets away from humidity. Use soft pouches for sunglasses and jewelry to avoid scratches.

3. Store In a Dry Box

Keep your watch in a soft pouch or box when not in use. Avoid humid storage spaces to reduce moisture-related damage.

4. Use Occasion-Based Rotation

If you own multiple watches, rotate between luxury and daily pieces. This reduces wear pressure on one single watch.

5. Check Lock and Crown Monthly

Inspect clasp lock, strap pins, and crown alignment once every month for better long-term reliability.

Final Tip

Choose watches according to use-case. Glamtreasure offers both luxury and everyday options so your premium pieces stay reserved for occasions and daily watches handle routine wear.
